Full Wax & Detail

With our expert waxing and detailing service, restore the gleam. We thoroughly clean, polish, and apply premium marine wax to shield your boat's paint against oxidation, sun damage, and salt. Every surface, from steel to gelcoat, receives the care it requires to look its best, leaving your boat safe, shiny, and ready to attract attention.

Cabin Cleaning

With our expert cabin cleaning service, take pleasure in a pristine interior. We provide your living areas a clean, welcoming, and cruise-ready feel by doing everything from deep carpet and upholstery treatments to head and galley sanitizing.

Wet Sand & Compound

Wetsanding and compounding are steps in our restoration process that eliminate oxidation, scratches, and surface imperfections, rejuvenating your boat's appearance and giving it a brand-new shimmer.

Boat Washing

Use our expert boat wash service to keep your vessel looking its best. We clean the hull, deck, and other surfaces from top to bottom of accumulation, dirt, and salt. To preserve your finish and maintain your boat ready for cruise, we utilize marine-safe products.

Ceramic Coating

This unique coating produces a glossy, smooth surface that keeps dirt from adhering, repels water, and blocks UV rays. It makes cleaning easier and prolongs the time your boat looks brand new. In this manner, you can spend more time on the water and less time cleaning.

Management

We take care of everything, including crew coordination, system checks, and regular maintenance and cleaning. This guarantees that your boat is always in excellent condition and prepared for action when you are. We maintain the proper operation of your yacht whether you are on board or not
