Yacht Management

 Our all-inclusive yacht management services are made to ensure that your vessel is always prepared for inter-island travel, personal usage, or charter.

We manage all aspects of vessel care, from regular maintenance to emergency repairs and preventive maintenance, all of which are customized to the particular requirements of Bahamian waters. To keep your yacht in top shape, we collaborate with a carefully selected network of reliable local experts, including shipwrights, qualified electricians, marine mechanics, and restoration specialists, for everything from little repairs to comprehensive systems oversight.

With service standards that are on par with or better than worldwide best practices (ABYC, RINA, or ISO), Seaworthy Solutions coordinates and completes all work in accordance with the instructions of the Bahamas Maritime Authority (BMA) and the Bahamian Port Authority, so that you can concentrate on having fun at sea, let us take care of the coordination, compliance, and logistics.
